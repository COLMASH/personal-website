# Santana AI — Brand Style Guide

Use this document as reference when generating any visual content, presentations, documents, or designs for Santana AI.

---

## Brand Identity

**Company:** Santana AI
**Industry:** AI Consulting & Solutions
**Tone:** Modern, clean, professional, tech-forward. Confident but approachable. Never flashy or cluttered.

---

## Colors

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Primary Accent** | `#00b162` | Buttons, links, highlights, headings accents, logo "AI" text |
| **Primary Hover** | `#009048` | Hover states, darker accent contexts |
| **Background** | `#fafcfa` | Page backgrounds, surfaces |
| **Text** | `#1a1a1a` | Body text, headings |
| **Text Muted** | `#6b7280` | Secondary text, captions, footnotes |
| **Border** | `#e5e7eb` | Dividers, table borders, card outlines |
| **Accent Light** | `rgba(0, 177, 98, 0.08)` | Subtle tinted backgrounds (badges, highlights) |

**Rules:**
- The green accent (`#00b162`) is the dominant brand color. Use it sparingly for maximum impact — headings borders, key CTAs, and highlights only.
- Never use the accent as a large background fill. Use the light tint (`rgba(0,177,98,0.08)`) instead.
- Body backgrounds should always be off-white (`#fafcfa`) or white (`#ffffff`), never dark.

---

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Source |
|------|------|--------|--------|
| **Body / UI** | Inter | 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold) | Google Fonts |
| **Headings / Display** | Playfair Display | 600, 700 | Google Fonts |

**Rules:**
- Use Playfair Display exclusively for headings (h1, h2) and display text. Never for body copy.
- Use Inter for everything else: paragraphs, labels, buttons, captions, table text.
- Body text size: 15–16px. Line height: 1.7.
- Heading sizes: h1 ~2.4rem, h2 ~1.5rem. Line height: 1.25–1.3.
- Letter spacing on the logo: `0.18em`, uppercase.

---

## Logo

The Santana AI logo is purely typographic — no icon or symbol.

**Format:** `SANTANA` in bold dark text (`#1a1a1a`) + `AI` in brand accent (`#00b162`)
**Font:** Inter, Extra Bold (800), uppercase, letter-spacing 0.18em
**Variations:**
- Full: `SANTANA AI`
- The "AI" portion is always in the accent green
- Minimum size: 14px font-size for legibility

**Do:**
- Always keep "SANTANA" and "AI" together on the same line
- Maintain generous spacing around the logo

**Don't:**
- Never use an icon or symbol with the logo
- Never change the colors (no white-on-green, no all-green)
- Never use a different font for the logo

---

## Visual Style

- **Border radius:** 10px for cards, containers, and buttons
- **Shadows:** Subtle, layered. Example: `0 20px 60px -15px rgba(0,0,0,0.2)`
- **Dividers:** 1px solid `#e5e7eb`
- **Accent borders:** 4px solid `#00b162` on the left side of headings
- **Buttons:** Rounded-full (pill shape), accent background, white text
- **Badges:** Light accent background with accent text, rounded-full

---

## Slide / Presentation Guidelines

When creating slides or presentations for Santana AI:

1. **Background:** White (`#ffffff`) or off-white (`#fafcfa`)
2. **Title slides:** Use Playfair Display for the main title, Inter for subtitles
3. **Accent usage:** Thin green line or left-border as visual separator — not large green blocks
4. **Text hierarchy:** Dark headings, muted body text, accent for key terms only
5. **Charts/graphs:** Use `#00b162` as the primary data color, `#6b7280` for secondary
6. **Logo placement:** Top-left or bottom-center, small, never dominant
7. **Overall feel:** Minimal, spacious, sophisticated — let the content breathe
